TUMBBAD

Tumbbad is a 2018 Indian Hindi-language[4] folk horror film directed by Rahi Anil Barve.[1] Additionally, Anand Gandhi served as the creative director,[5] and Adesh Prasad served as the co-director.[5] Written by Mitesh Shah, Prasad, Barve, and Gandhi,[1] the film was produced by Sohum Shah, Aanand L. Rai, Mukesh Shah and Amita Shah. Starring Sohum Shah in the lead role as Vinayak Rao, it follows the story of his search for a hidden treasure in the 20th century India the village of Tumbbad, Maharashtra. The categorization of Tumbbad as either a Bollywood or Marathi film has been a topic of discussion among filmmakers. In an interview, Rahi Anil Barve.[1], the filmmaker, clarified that Tumbbad is fundamentally a Marathi film that captures the intricacies of its settings. Despite being made in Hindi, it is partially produced and distributed by Eros International to ensure a broader reach among the Hindi-speaking audience.
